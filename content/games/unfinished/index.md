Unfinished was an opportunity to make some pixel art, and I added a story around it for PixoJam 2026. The pixel art took me a few days and I put the game together in a few hours. The theme was "give me scroll," and I took advantage of the Scroll Text feature of RPG Maker to add a little flavor and worldbuilding to my pixel art. 

I like leaving crumbs of details and the forcing the player to use their imagination to fill in the gaps. What is the player character? Why is there a clock and what happens when the time runs out? Am I supposed to feel lonely or comforted by these memories? Is there a world beyond this rectangle? 

Inspired by concepts from Disco Elysium, Loop Hero, and other nihilism related fiction.
